Privacy Policy — Sky Hopper

Effective date: 6 July 2026 • App package: com.B2Cube.skyhopper

Sky Hopper ("the game", "we") is a casual arcade game published by Nautilus. This policy explains what information the game handles and your choices. The short version: we do not collect, store, or sell your personal information. The game has no account system and works offline. Advertising is provided by Google AdMob, which processes limited device data described below.

Information we collect

None. Sky Hopper does not require registration and does not transmit your gameplay data anywhere. Your progress (scores, coins, unlocked cosmetics, settings) is stored only in the app's private storage on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the game.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The game shows advertising provided by Google AdMob (Google LLC). To serve and measure ads, Google may collect and process device information, including your device's Advertising ID, IP address, coarse general location derived from IP, and app interaction data (e.g. that an ad was viewed). This processing happens under Google's own policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses advertising data

Ad placements in Sky Hopper are limited by design: banners appear only in menus, interstitials appear only between game runs, and rewarded ads play only when you explicitly choose to watch one.

Your choices and consent

  • EEA / UK users: on first launch you are shown a consent form (Google User Messaging Platform). You can change your choice at any time in Settings → Privacy & ad consent options inside the game.
  • Personalised ads: you can reset or delete your Advertising ID in your device's settings (Settings → Google → Ads), which limits ad personalisation across all apps.
  • Child mode: the game includes an optional child mode (Settings) that requests child-directed treatment and restricts ads to a G ("General audiences") content rating.

Children

Sky Hopper is a general-audience game and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. When child mode is enabled, ad requests are tagged for child-directed treatment in accordance with COPPA and Google Play Families policies.

Data sharing and selling

We do not sell, trade, or share any personal information, because we do not collect any. The only third party that processes data in connection with the game is Google (for advertising), as described above.

Data retention and deletion

All game data lives on your device. Uninstalling the game permanently deletes it. We hold nothing to delete on our side.

Security

Game data is stored in Android's app-private storage, inaccessible to other apps on standard devices.

Changes to this policy

If the game's data practices ever change (for example, adding online leaderboards), this policy will be updated and the effective date revised before the change ships.
